Mood: Facial Recognition


Welcome to Mood, a facial expression recognizer

Upon hitting upload you will be asked to upload an image.
Based on your image, a youtube video matching the mood of your photo,
analyzed via face recognition, will be displayed.

1.Smile - Happy Music Search
2.Neutral - Neutral Music Search
3.Random Face (Other Expressions) - Alternative Music Search
4.No Face Detected - Nujabes (Chill Pop) music search
